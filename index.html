What is the process of Data analysis?

The data analysis process begins with defining clear objectives and collecting relevant data. This data is cleaned to remove inaccuracies and explored using statistical summaries and visualizations. Statistical and machine learning methods are then applied to model the data and derive insights. These insights are communicated to stakeholders to inform business decisions, and the process is continuously monitored and refined for improved accuracy and relevance.”.
